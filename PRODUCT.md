# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Adults managing both mental and physical health — often after a stressful week, a doctor visit, or a quiet late-night moment when they want clarity without booking an appointment. They need a private place to log mood, vitals, and thoughts, and gentle AI support when a human therapist isn't available.

## Product Purpose

HealthMind unifies mood tracking, journaling, vitals (BP/glucose), therapy modules, and AI conversation into one calm companion. Success is when someone opens the app and feels oriented — not sold to — and can log or reflect in under a minute.

## Brand Personality

Friendly health companion — warm cream paper, bold blue action, lime sparks of energy. Feels like Alan: serious about care, light in tone. Not a lab brochure, not a spa, not a startup pitch deck.

## Anti-references

- Purple-on-white / indigo-glow AI SaaS landing pages
- Forest-green / moss wellness templates
- Hims lifestyle photo monoculture
- Cold hospital blue-on-white brochures
- Fake celebrity testimonials and mascot-led heroes (Alan has those; we don't)

## Design Principles

1. **Brand-first hero** — HealthMind + one promise + two CTAs; product preview follows.
2. **One soft sans** — Alan Sans for everything; personality in weight, not font pairing.
3. **Tinted panels, not cards with shadows** — blue / green / lime washes like Alan.
4. **Big simple stats** — proof without fake quotes.
5. **Motion like a smile** — short fades only.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum for text contrast. Support `prefers-reduced-motion`. Avoid relying on color alone for mood/vitals meaning. Language stays inclusive and non-stigmatizing around mental health.
